# TallyTrack – Smart Inventory Management System for Small Retail Businesses

## Overview

**TallyTrack** is a web-based inventory management system designed to help small retail businesses efficiently monitor and manage their inventory operations. The system provides real-time tracking of products, stock movements, transactions, and inventory levels, enabling business owners to make informed decisions and streamline daily operations.

By automating inventory processes and providing visual business insights through analytics and reports, TallyTrack reduces manual work, minimizes inventory errors, and improves overall business efficiency.

---

## Objectives

* Simplify inventory management for small retail businesses.
* Monitor stock levels in real time.
* Improve accuracy in stock-in and stock-out transactions.
* Maintain organized records of products and transactions.
* Provide business insights through automated reports and graphical analytics.
* Reduce inventory shortages and overstocking issues.

---

## Key Features

### Product Management

Users can:

* Add, update, and remove products.
* Organize products into categories.
* Manage product information such as names, descriptions, prices, and quantities.

### Stock-In Management

The system allows users to:

* Record incoming inventory.
* Update stock quantities automatically.
* Track restocking activities.
* Maintain accurate inventory records.

### Stock-Out Management

Users can:

* Process product withdrawals or sales.
* Automatically deduct quantities from inventory.
* Maintain accurate stock availability records.
* Prevent inventory discrepancies.

### Real-Time Inventory Tracking

Business owners can monitor:

* Current stock availability.
* Remaining inventory quantities.
* Product movement history.
* Low-stock items requiring replenishment.

### Category Management

Products can be grouped into categories, making inventory organization and management more efficient.

### Transaction Logs

The system maintains comprehensive records of:

* Stock-in transactions
* Stock-out transactions
* Product updates
* Inventory adjustments

This provides transparency and accountability for all inventory activities.

### Activity and Audit Logs

Every important system action is recorded, allowing administrators to:

* Track user activities.
* Review inventory changes.
* Monitor operational history.
* Improve data security and accountability.

### Business Analytics Dashboard

TallyTrack generates automated visual reports, including:

* Inventory distribution by category
* Stock movement trends
* Product performance statistics
* Inventory level summaries
* Business operation insights

Interactive graphs and charts help business owners quickly understand inventory performance and make data-driven decisions.

### ⚠️ Low Stock Monitoring

The system automatically identifies products that are running low in stock, helping businesses replenish inventory before shortages occur.
